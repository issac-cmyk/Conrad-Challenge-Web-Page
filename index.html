Solution Comparison

Our experiment aims to quantify if our wearable spinal assist device can effectively reduce mechanical load on the L4-L5 lumbar segment during trunk flexion. Since direct disc pressure measurement is invasive, we use the device's support force as a non-invasive proxy for reduced disc load.

Features Traditional Lumbar Support Physical Therapy Our Solution
